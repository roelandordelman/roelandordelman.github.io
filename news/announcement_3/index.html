DARIAH Annual Event and DHBenelux 2022

May 31, 2022

2022

This week we present our CLARIAH work on the DARIAH annual event and the DHBenelux conference.

DARIAH:

  • Data Stories in CLARIAH — Developing a Research Infrastructure for Storytelling with Heritage and Culture Data (Roeland Ordelman, Willemien Sanders, Richard Zijdeman, Rana Klein, Julia Noordegraaf, Jasmijn Van Gorp, Mari Wigham, Menzo Windhouwer) -- poster sessions on Wednesday and Thursday
Copied this slide from the Panel Session on Data-Driven storytelling through FAIR data (Sharif Islam, Andreas Weber, Sally Chambers, Erzsébet Tóth-Czifra, Kerstin Arnold, Henning Scholz), as a reminder of perspectives in the context of data stories and research infrastructure.

DHBenelux:

  • Developing Data Stories as Enhanced Publications in Digital Humanities (Willemien Sanders, Roeland Ordelman, Mari Wigham, Rana Klein, Jasmijn van Gorp and Julia Noordegraaf) -- oral presentation by Willemien on Wednesday
  • Enriching Cultural Heritage Data for Research – the Quest for Interoperability in Audiovisual Archives (Mari Wigham, Willem Melder and Roeland Ordelman) -- oral presentation by Mari on Friday
